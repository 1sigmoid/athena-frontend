Why Athena?


1Athena is a platform that allows students to find events, competitions and classes based on their interests, rather than their existing social circle, in order to give every student an equal opportunity to develop their skills.



Want to watch our full pitch? Check it out here on YouTube

A Repository and Showcase for your all your Achievements

Store all your extracurricular accolades and certificates safely with us, so that you always have access to them. As Athena is a student focused site, you can also share information about your accolades with family and friends, allowing you to display the fruits of your hard work with elegance and ease.

Events based on what you like

Were you always interested in something, but didn't have the necessary social connections to follow through? Not anymore. With Athena, we show you events based on your interests, rather than your existing social connections. Our proprietary DaVinci Recommendation Engine uses 17 unique data points to recommend the highest quality events.


Open Source and Privacy Focused

Our mission is not to profit from providing opportunities. That is why, we have open sourced all our code on GitHub to allow anyone to build upon and improve Athena. We also only collect the minimum amount of data required to bring you the optimal experience.

Easy Advertising For Organizers

Do you have trouble advertising your event to the right audience? With Athena, you can be sure that your event does not go unnoticed and has the highest turnout possible. Advertise your events to the right groups, and enroll interested candidates directly from our platform!
